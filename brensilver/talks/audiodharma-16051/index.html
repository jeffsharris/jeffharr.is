Lightly Guided Meditation

AudioDharma 36:27 2022-04-10

This talk begins as a lightly guided meditation, using ambient sound, breath, and body awareness to settle the room. Matthew Brensilver then turns toward how the mind keeps wanting to optimize experience, and he gently explores the relief of not sorting every moment into pleasant and unpleasant. The long middle and ending section becomes a quiet, repetitive accompaniment that supports staying with simple presence, though parts of the transcript appear unclear.
