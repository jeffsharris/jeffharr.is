Guided Medtiation: Checking Hindrances, Checking Defilements

AudioDharma 28:30 2025-04-30

This guided meditation begins with settling into the body and then scans the five hindrances as a practical pulse check: restlessness, dullness, craving, aversion, and doubt. The emphasis stays gentle and observational, with repeated invitations to notice what is present without forcing anything away. As the practice unfolds, the talk turns toward relaxing the effort to control experience and allowing attention to become more open, unmixed, and receptive.

Chapters

  1. 00:12 Settling In - The meditation opens by landing in the body and establishing a steady, receptive ground for practice.
  2. 02:20 Scanning Hindrances - The teacher introduces the five hindrances and invites a simple check-in with whatever is present.
  3. 05:19 Bearing Unpleasantness - The focus turns to how unpleasantness is met in the nervous system and how awareness can hold it without judgment.
  4. 09:40 Choosing an Anchor - Different possible anchors are named, from breath and body to sound and open awareness.
  5. 12:05 Unmixed Attention - The guidance emphasizes relaxing, receiving experience, and letting attention bow rather than grasp.
  6. 17:33 Letting Go Of Control - The talk points toward surrendering control and noticing that goodness does not need to be manufactured.
  7. 21:47 Putting Down Yardsticks - The closing movement gestures toward releasing comparison and measurement, and simply resting with what is here.
