Matthew Brensilver Dharma Talks

Guided Meditation: Openness of Awareness

AudioDharma 29:27 2023-02-16

What changes when attention stops rehearsing agitation and instead settles into breath, brightness, and release? The talk moves from a simple reminder that how we attend shapes experience into a guided practice of relaxing the body, welcoming the breath, and sensing the inhale and exhale as energizing, soothing, and eventually spacious. As the practice deepens, it shifts from a smaller, protected feeling to something like the openness of awareness, while also noting how rest can change the way hope, fear, and the loose ends of life are seen.

  6. 13:15 Seclusion in Breath - Brensilver describes how attention can gather into a protected, secluded world and reveal the openness of awareness.
  7. 19:57 Hope and Fear - The teaching shifts from spaciousness to the ways hope and fear entangle the heart with worldly concerns.
  8. 23:57 Closing Reflection - The talk briefly returns to the same contrast between embroilment and the quieter clarity found in breath and simplicity.
