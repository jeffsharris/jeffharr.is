Matthew Brensilver Dharma Talks

Formal Opening Part 1 (sit)

AudioDharma 18:37 2024-08-28

A brief opening for retreat, centered on beginning with a 15-minute sit and then moving through logistics for the days ahead. Matthew Brensilver covers seating, hearing assistance, the basic alternation of sitting and walking meditation, and the schedule for instruction, talks, reflections, and discussion groups. The talk ends by naming the value of everyone having set aside time for retreat.

Chapters

  1. 00:00 Opening the retreat - The retreat begins with a short invitation to sit and bless the time together through practice.
  2. 15:10 Comfort and seating - Practical guidance on hearing devices, cushions, chairs, and making the body more workable for practice.
  3. 16:32 Daily meditation rhythm - The basic rhythm is outlined: alternating silent sitting and walking meditation through the day.
  4. 16:58 Instruction and talks - He names the main touchpoints in the schedule, including morning instruction, the afternoon Dharma talk, and the evening reflection.
  5. 17:08 Practice groups - Participants are told how the discussion groups will work and that they will meet with both teachers over the course of the retreat.
  6. 17:35 Bell practice - Those ringing the bells are invited to practice beforehand, with a light touch preferred.
  7. 18:05 Closing encouragement - The opening closes with a check for questions and a simple appreciation for the time everyone has set aside.
