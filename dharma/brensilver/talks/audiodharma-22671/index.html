Guided Meditation: Relax and Let go

AudioDharma 29:27 2025-04-04

Begin by settling the body and taking a few deeper breaths, then let the breath become less of a project and more of a place to rest. The talk moves from gentle posture instructions into a quiet argument for renouncing the impulse to solve life, with the poignancy of mortality helping that letting go feel possible. From there, the practice keeps returning to what is restful—the settled body, silence, the space behind closed eyes, and the soothing of Buddha, Dharma, and Sangha—before opening into long stretches of simple counting and breath awareness.
