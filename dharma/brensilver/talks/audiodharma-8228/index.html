Matthew Brensilver Dharma Talks

Practice Note

AudioDharma 03:18 2017-09-20

Matthew Brensilver offers a brief practice note on what mindfulness can actually do: even when attention meets difficulty, something in the heart tends to relax. He speaks candidly about mindfulness as a real turning toward the moment, not a half-hearted technique, and suggests that awareness can bring a sense of inner safety and surrender. The talk ends by inviting listeners to notice the part of being that is soothed by awareness itself.
