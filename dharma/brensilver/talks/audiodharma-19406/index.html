Guided Meditation: Renouncing the Narrative Impulse

AudioDharma 30:15 2023-12-12

What happens when feeling arises and the mind immediately turns it into a story, a diagnosis, or a command? This guided meditation keeps returning to the body and the feeling tone itself, inviting the listener to notice mood, arousal, and the urge to narrate without following that impulse. Over time, the practice softens the compulsive push to figure things out and opens a little space where nothing needs to happen right now.
