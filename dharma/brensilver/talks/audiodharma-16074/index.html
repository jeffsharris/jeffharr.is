Matthew Brensilver Dharma Talks

Lightly Guided Meditation

AudioDharma 29:56 2022-04-12

The talk begins with a simple, steady invitation to practice together: full, deep breaths, a softening exhale, and attention brought down into the belly. From there, the guidance keeps returning to the body’s “densities” or pockets of intensity, suggesting that the breath can smooth them out without trying to solve the rest of life. The emphasis is on patient care—softening the face, jaw, eyes, and belly, and letting thinking become less urgent as the body is met with attention and warmth.
