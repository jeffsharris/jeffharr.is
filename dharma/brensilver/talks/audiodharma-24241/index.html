Guided Meditation: Ease Makes Letting Go Safer

AudioDharma 26:12 2025-11-19

The practice begins with finding a breath that carries a signal of ease into the body, then letting that ease coexist with whatever agitation is still present. Rather than trying to force discomfort away, the talk keeps returning to the places that are already soothed, asking attention to rest there and let awareness do its quieter work. From that steadier vantage, the future feels less like a problem to solve and more like something met by taking good care of what is here now. The meditation closes by settling into a subtle warmth, perhaps even the smallest smile, and recognizing that simply abiding is enough.
