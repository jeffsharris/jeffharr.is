Matthew Brensilver Dharma Talks

Guided Meditation: It's all come to this

AudioDharma 29:10 2024-12-04

Matthew Brensilver offers a quiet guided meditation centered on gathering the goodness of practice and meeting the present moment without violence. The talk moves from recollecting years of practice and the pain and wisdom that have led here, into a gentler way of relating to breath, body, and mind. He then turns toward refuge, showing how to rest in what is already here rather than trying to outthink uncertainty.

Chapters

  1. 00:12 Settling In - He welcomes listeners and invites them to settle, alert but comfortable, for meditation.
  2. 01:07 Recollecting Practice - The meditation begins by recalling moments of willingness, retreat, practice, and friendship with the Dharma.
  3. 04:08 All Come To This - He gathers the whole arc of practice and life—pain, greed, hate, wisdom, and love—into the present moment.
  4. 05:38 Gently Tending Life - A long stretch of repeated phrases emphasizes tenderness, acceptance, and non-violence toward experience.
  5. 13:58 Ordinary Strategies - He names the ways we try to manage uncertainty through pleasure, planning, and rehashing the past.
  6. 16:46 Deeper Refuge - The meditation shifts toward refuge, resting in breath, body, and the present life as it is.
  7. 24:06 Winds Of Change - He closes by inviting trust in impermanence, letting change move through without harm.
