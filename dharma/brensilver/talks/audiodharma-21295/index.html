Guided Meditation: Tranquility

AudioDharma 28:09 2024-09-18

How does the body settle when it has been trained to treat everything as urgent? This guided meditation keeps returning to the breath, the spine, the belly, and the felt sense of gravity, asking you to soften out of the head and into a steadier ground. From there, the practice gently works with sleepy or restless energy, moments of hyper-arousal, and the loose ends of the day, until tranquility is less an idea than a shared field the body can inhabit. When there is even a thread of pleasantness, the talk suggests following it and letting ease and tranquility feed each other.

  6. 10:38 Urgency and false alarms - The talk reframes everyday agitation as something to acknowledge without automatically obeying.
  7. 15:58 Accountability without blame - He encourages radical but blameless attention to what body and mind are doing moment by moment.
  8. 21:46 Following pleasantness - The meditation shifts toward any thread of comfort, inviting the listener to rest in it and let tranquility grow.
