Guided Medtiation: Checking Hindrances, Checking Defilements

AudioDharma 28:30 2025-04-30

The practice starts with a simple body landing, then moves into a careful scan of the five hindrances: restlessness, sloth, desire, aversion, and doubt. Rather than treating them as failures, the talk frames them as ordinary signals in the nervous system and invites a quiet “pulse check” of whatever is present. From there, the guidance keeps returning to how attention can get mixed with wanting, and how to soften into an unmixed awareness that bows rather than grasps. The talk closes by pointing toward surrender, with less effort to manufacture goodness and more willingness to let it be found.
