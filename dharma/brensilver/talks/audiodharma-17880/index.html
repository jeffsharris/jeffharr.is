Matthew Brensilver Dharma Talks

Guided Meditation: Stability and Change

AudioDharma 29:20 2023-03-09

This guided meditation settles into the body, the breath, and the felt support of the earth before opening into a reflection on stability and change. Matthew Brensilver gradually shifts from simple instructions to a quieter contemplation of impermanence, asking what it means to rest in awareness without trying to hold experience still. The talk ends by widening into a river image: life as continuous flow, with brief moments of solidifying and release.

Chapters

  1. 00:41 Settling Together - The meditation begins by inviting the listener to sit down and let the mind and body arrive.
  2. 02:11 Resting In Awareness - Thoughts and feelings are allowed to come to rest and take their place within awareness.
  3. 04:42 Gravity And Breath - The guidance turns toward bodily support, gravity, and the breath as steadying forces.
  4. 08:41 Everything In Motion - The talk opens into a direct contemplation of change, impermanence, and the lack of fixed things.
  5. 10:27 Refuge And Center - Matthew names refuge, the center line, and a kind of inner safety that can hold changing conditions.
  6. 13:12 Flexible Love - The meditation points to a love that is pliable enough to absorb the body blows of being human.
  7. 17:10 River Of Life - The closing reflection uses the image of a river with no banks, inviting a willingness to join the flow.
  8. 23:35 Open Mesh Of Being - A quoted teaching about the openness of experience deepens the image of permeability and release.
