Guided Meditation Presence is not a Transaction

AudioDharma 29:56 2025-08-19

Start by settling the body well enough for a kind of truce with its imperfection, then let the breath and the closed-eye stillness signal that this moment is good enough to begin letting go. The talk keeps returning to a simple but demanding move: notice how the mind keeps propping up stories, fears, and control, and see the relief in setting some of that down. Midway through, the teaching sharpens into receiving experience in a non-transactional way—without trying to extract something from the moment—and trusting the moment as a teacher or medicine. It closes by widening the frame: meditation is not placed beside life, but life itself is held within the Dharma.
