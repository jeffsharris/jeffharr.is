Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Guided Meditation 1

Dharma Seed 25:40 2014-12-07

The practice begins with posture and a quiet body scan, softening tension in the face, throat, shoulders, belly, and hands before widening attention to the whole seated field. From there, the talk keeps returning to a simple movement: open the awareness again and again, feel the body as an energy space, and notice what helps that space feel bright, relaxed, and alive. It then introduces optional images—lines of energy through the center and down the legs, the breath as a gentle expansion and contraction, and even the body as translucent light—while reminding listeners to keep only what feels useful.

Chapters

  1. 00:00 Settling the posture - The meditation begins by establishing an upright but relaxed posture and sensing a balance of wakefulness and softness.
  2. 01:18 Releasing tension - Attention moves through the face, throat, neck, shoulders, back, chest, and belly, encouraging gentle relaxation and release.
  3. 02:53 Grounding in contact - The practice widens to simple sensations in the hands, backside, legs, and feet, grounding awareness in immediate bodily contact.
  4. 03:48 Opening the field - Awareness is invited to expand beyond the body into a larger space filled with bright, sensitive presence.
  5. 05:37 Restoring spacious presence - The talk emphasizes repeatedly re-expanding attention when it shrinks, using a wind-and-sail image to support an open, filled space.
  6. 09:02 Imagining energy lines - A central line of energy is introduced, then extended into additional lines down the legs, with an emphasis on sensing what feels supportive.
  7. 11:43 Breath and support - The meditation offers the breath as an optional support for the whole-body field, including gentle expansion on the in-breath and relaxation on the out-breath.
  8. 15:18 Experimenting with form - Listeners are encouraged to try different combinations of breath, energy lines, and imagination, always returning to the felt sense of the whole space.
  9. 19:00 Body of light - The practice deepens into a radiant or translucent body of light, with color and luminosity left open to the listener's own response.
