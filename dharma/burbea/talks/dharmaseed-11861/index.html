Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Opening Talk for Work Retreat - Working and Awakening

Dharma Seed 37:03 2008-03-05

(Gaia House) Opening talk for a retreat that looks at how we may create artificial divisions between what seems 'sacred' and what seems 'worldly'; between 'retreat life' and 'everyday life', 'meditation' and work' and so seek transformation through only a small part of our lives. In opening to a profound wisdom that sees beyond such dualities, we can come to know an authentic freedom that is truly boundless.
