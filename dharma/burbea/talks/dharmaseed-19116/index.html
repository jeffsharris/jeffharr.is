Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Dharma practice and the climate Crisis

Dharma Seed 61:12 2013-04-17

(Spirit Rock Meditation Center) At the time of Earth Day, we pose a a dilemma and challenge. Dharma practice is so compelling. The climate crisis is so compelling. Which do we choose? Both, in our own ways. We look at the ways that our practice (and principles of generosity, compassion, ethics, interdependence, and wisdom) call us to regard and that those responding deeply need spiritual practices and principles to meet the great need.
