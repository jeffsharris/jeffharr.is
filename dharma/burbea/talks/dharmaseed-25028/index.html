The Four Noble Truths of Responding to Climate Change

Dharma Seed 66:01 2014-10-08

(Spirit Rock Meditation Center) Continuing our exploration of ethical practice during earth care week. We examine, through the frame work of the Four Noble Truths, the question of the nature of climate change and how to respond as individuals, as communities, nationally and internationally.
