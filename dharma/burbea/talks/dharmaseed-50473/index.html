Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Aspects of the Imaginal (Part 3)

Dharma Seed 56:47 2017-12-17

In this talk, Rob Burbea continues refining the idea of the imaginal by stressing that it is relational rather than something any image or object possesses on its own. He develops the image of a lattice or constellation of linked factors—especially energy body awareness, soul-making, and love—showing how attention to one node can awaken the others. The talk stays practical throughout, balancing disciplined attention with a sense that imaginal life also involves grace, newness, and something not fully under technique.

Chapters

  1. 00:00 Recap of the framework - He reviews the loose, non-exhaustive list of imaginal aspects and emphasizes that the teaching is meant to sharpen discernment without becoming rigid.
  2. 06:07 Relational, not inherent - Using James Hillman as a comparison, he argues that nothing is inherently imaginal; it becomes so through a particular relationship and what that relationship opens.
  3. 10:33 The imaginal lattice - He introduces the image of a lattice of nodes, explaining that when enough elements are alive together, the whole perception can become imaginal.
  4. 19:10 Wiggling a node - He gives practical suggestions for nudging practice along the spectrum, especially by noticing and enlivening the energy body as one node in the constellation.
  5. 26:53 What energy body means - He defines energy body as a way of looking, contrasts it with materialist conceptions, and shows how body, concept, image, and felt sense overlap.
  6. 37:17 Soul-making and jhana - He turns to soul-making, saying it involves eros, psyche, and logos, and illustrates it with jhana practice and the deepening sense of connection to the tradition.
  7. 43:18 New territory and play - He explores how newness, body awareness, heart practice, and playfulness can themselves become soul-making when they open fresh range and meaning.
  8. 52:22 Love as a key node - He ends by focusing on loving and being loved as a decisive node in the imaginal lattice, including the different flavors love can take and the value of affirming it.
