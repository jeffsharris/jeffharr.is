Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Sense Desire

Dharma Seed 59:34 2008-11-14

(Gaia House) We exist and move in the world of sense contacts, and yet often we neglect to examine this relationship very deeply, or it becomes just another way to judge ourselves. Can we challenge our assumptions, habits and views and inquire caringly in this area in order to open to a more profound and unexpected freedom?
