The Anatomy of Ignorance 2 - The Social Roots of Ignorance and climate Disruption

Dharma Seed 64:06 2013-10-02

(Spirit Rock Meditation Center) For Earth Care Week we review the three aspects of ignorance examined last week - personal (or psychological), social, and universal. We focus on the social roots of ignorance with attention mostly to understanding and responding to the climate crisis.
