Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Climate Change, Capitalism, and Buddhadharma

Dharma Seed 57:26 2013-11-21

(Insight Meditation Community of Berkeley) An impassioned talk that includes a little known story from the suttas about the dangers of and suffering caused by the notion of private property. During the talk, winds of 45 mph buffeted the monastery, emergency sirens sounded in the distance, and three power outages occurred. Ends with a lively discussion with the sangha.
