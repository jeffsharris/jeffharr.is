Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

Seeing the System You Are Part Of

Dharma Seed 59:40 2012-06-28

(Insight Meditation Community of Berkeley) Sharing a positive vision for the future from Ernest Callenbach’s <a href="http://www.commondreams.org/view/2012/05/07-0">"Epistle to Ecotopians"</a>. We then begin to explore the first of five Dharma principles using systems and sustainability expert Bob Doppelt’s book <i>From Me to We</i>. The ignorance of these principles is what perpetuates the problem and the understanding of them is the key to changing our consciousness and providing a path toward healing the planet. This is part 2 of the series 'Appropriate Response at the Tipping Point' that began June 14.
