Rob Burbea Dharma Talks

A Question of Faith

Dharma Seed 60:10 2007-11-08

(Gaia House) A probing exploration of the movements of faith in our practice. Whether we are conscious of it or not, we are always placing our faith somewhere. What do we have faith in? What shapes our sense of limitation or possibility? And how do faith and confidence deepen?
